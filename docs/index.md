# Ictonyx

**Iteration Comparison Testing Over N-runs: Yield eXamination**

Ictonyx trains your model N times under statistically independent seeds,
collects the full distribution of outcomes, and applies rigorous
non-parametric statistics to reason about performance distributions —
rather than treating a single training run as a measurement.
